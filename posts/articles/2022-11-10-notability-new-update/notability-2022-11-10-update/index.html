    Notability 2022-11-10 Update

    Today is 2022-11-10, 2 days ago Notability updated a new version (Version 11.8) involves a high-demanding feature – Night version.

    Programmers and researchers spend a lot of time on screens, especially for people use iPad to take notes. Staring at the screen for a long time is harmful, whereas using a dark background and reduce the harmness. Personally, I use PDF Expert as my primary PDF viewer, and the reason is simply it have the so-called “dark mode” option. It simply changes the white to black and black to white, and this revert color will release the pressure on your eyes.

    I am looking for a software that can mimic this feature for such a long time. What I demand not only requires a dark mode, but also enable me to switch between light and dark mode. For example, using white pen in dark mode should corresponding to using black pen in light mode, and the changing should be automatic! There is an app called CollaNote which have this feature, and it rewards 4.9/5.0 rating from over 1200 reviews from App store. However, this software is not maintained by a proper company. Personally speaking, I feel somehow laggy from using CollaNote, and therefore I quit this app.

    Notability’s new version has this feature and it can even change your background (such as PDF files) into dark mode (just like the above pictures). Now, you are able to writing in dark mode: The ability to change the underlying PDF file to dark mode as well is already impressive and not achievable by many other note-taking apps such as GoodNotes 5. It can seamlessly changing from dark mode to light mode: This is remarkable! Moreover, you can export your notes and annotation in its original form regardless which mode you are currenly at:

    This feature is incredible and making me feel worthy to pay for this app.
