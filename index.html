You are on Ansar's website, that's me on the right. I am a lecturer at Mathematics Department, Universitas Sulawesi Barat, Indonesia.

I have a strong passion for mathematics, particularly in the field of computational mathematics. Over the past three years, I have been deeply engaged in research on fixed-point iteration processes, exploring their applications in differential equations. Beyond my academic work, I am also committed to advancing mathematics education. I have organized and participated in social activities aimed at empowering mathematics teachers to enhance their teaching skills. These initiatives include training sessions on using tools like GeoGebra and Canva to create interactive and engaging learning experiences for students. Feel free to contact me for potential collaboration or discussion within my area of expertise.
